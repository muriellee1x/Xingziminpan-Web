TEXT

危楼高百尺
手可摘星辰
不敢高声语
恐惊天上人

ENGLISH TRANSLATION

Perilous rise hundred feet high
Reach and touch these stars so nearby
Keeping my voice down to a sigh
Lest I startle the dwellers in the sky
