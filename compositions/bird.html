A WANDERING BIRD 寻路的鸟
SSA and piano 4'30"
Text by Yang Chen | 词：陈阳
Performed by Guangzhou Little Petrel Children's Choir | 演出：广州小海燕合唱团
Conducted by Zhijun Diao | 指挥：刁志君
Xinghai Concert Hall, Guangzhou
2021.10.19
TEXT

树上落了一只迷路的鸟
一只沉醉于远行的鸟
春风让它忘记了世界之大
也忘记了自己的巢

这不是我的树 鸟说
我要寻找回家的路
你不是我的鸟 树说
但我愿给你一个怀抱

今夜 只此一夜
我给你讲个旅人的故事
待到清晨
我将与林中群莺
一同苏醒 一同欢鸣
去向不同的地方

故事里的旅人
在林中遇见了一只鸟
迷路的人啊
你可曾见过一棵缄默的树
一棵梦里满是柔情的树

今夜 只此一夜
我给你讲个鸟的故事
它在万水千山里
找到了另一个归宿

ENGLISH TRANSLATION

A wandering bird rests upon the tree
A traveller drunk on the winds so free
The spring breeze makes it forget the vast world
And the nest where its dreams once unfurled

"This isn't my tree," the bird does say
"I'm searching for the path to find my way."
"You're not my bird," the tree replies
"But I'll offer you a warm embrace to rise."

Tonight, just for this night
I'll share a tale of a traveller's flight
When dawn breaks, I'll wake with the larks
Together we'll sing in the morning's sparks
Heading to places far and bright

The traveller in the tale
Meets a bird in the woods
"O lost one, have you seen a tree
Silent and dreaming, full of tender glee?"

Tonight, just for this night
I'll tell you a tale of a bird in flight
Across mountains and rivers wide
It found another home where it can abide
