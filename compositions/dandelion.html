ENGLISH TRANSLATION

Fly, ever so tenderly.
The wind carries me over the mountains,
The wind guides me across the waters.
Fly, ever so slowly,
Over the hill, over the river,
Not knowing when to return.

Fly, ever so tenderly.
The distant lights are but a slur,
Only the murmuring stream reaches my ears.
Fly, ever so slowly,
In the quiet night, over those in sleep,
Knowing none who'd fly with me.

Suddenly the wind stills,
I drift down beside you,
How I long to linger here,
To end this endless journey.

But alas, the wind starts again,
Taking me away from the earth,
Flying toward the unknown.

Fly, ever so freely.
White clouds wander below me,
I have the breeze as my wings.
Fly, carried by the wind,
Fading away into the distance,
That mountain, that water,
Only in dreams can I return.

Fading away into the distance,
Only in dreams can I return.
