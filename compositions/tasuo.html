ENGLISH TRANSLATION

North of the wooden bridge, south of the lane,
Draped in hues of elegance, the garden's vibrant strain,
The river willows weave a fresh gown's refrain.

As rains depart, the awakening begins,
On old stone slabs, green moss softly spins,
New paint on red railings where history thins.

In warm blankets, we rise early to learn,
The gentle breeze outweighs a thousand books' turn,
With laughter and play, in floral fields we yearn.

By the banks of the river, the mandarin ducks wake,
Outside the wall, swings chime like laughter's stake,
At dusk we return, rocking boats on the lake.

Pillow dreams on white walls, drifting to sleep,
Sweet osmanthus porridge, memories we keep.
A single tile lifted, sweet fragrance to steep,
In the cup of Bi Luo, dreams gently sweep.

Timid dawn blushes, waters glimmer and sway,
Clouds vanish, the moon hides, stars flicker and play,
Neighbors rise early, washing clothes in the fray,
The green grass embraces the smoke of the day.

By the pond's flourishing branches, two swallows in flight,
With brush in hand, we paint new fans in delight.
With youth at my side, we roam the lake bright,
In Suzhou, all know that spring's warmth is right.
