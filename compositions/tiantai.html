TEXT

青冥通去路，谁见独随缘。
此地春前别，何山夜后禅。
石桥隐深树，朱阙见晴天。
好是修行处，师当住几年。

ENGLISH TRANSLATION

In the azure void, a path unfolds,
Who sees me tread, alone, where fate beholds?
Here in spring's embrace, we part our way,
What mountains guard the night's calm, silent stay?
Stone bridges hide 'neath trees that deepen shade,
Crimson towers glimpse the bright blue sky displayed.
What a fine place for cultivation's art,
My master should reside here, through the years to impart.
