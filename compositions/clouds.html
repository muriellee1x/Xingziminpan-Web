TEXT

穿透了 潇潇的雨
掠过了 隐隐的鸟
我当 握着你未送达的手稿
跃入云中 招摇

透明的尾 划破寂寥
我当 乘着它凛冽的气息
去向远方 不畏千里迢迢

在微光的背后沉睡
吞吐薄雾 脚踩松涛 山水茫茫
抛下半句呼喊 回音渺渺
不问归期 归期尚早 如何归去 无人知晓
星辰总要暗淡 总要飘散 总要离开怀抱

至少 可以在坠落前
嗅满风的味道
至少 可以朝着晨曦
把门轻敲
至少 可以藏一片湛蓝 说
我曾在云中

ENGLISH TRANSLATION

Piercing through the drizzling rain
Gliding past the distant birds
I hold
Your undelivered letter
Leap into the clouds
and sway

The transparent tail
Cuts through the silence
I ride
On its biting breath
To faraway shores
Fearless of the length ahead

Sleeping behind the shimmering light
Breathing in the mists
Feet on the pine waves
Mountains and rivers blur
I cast away half a shout
Echoes faintly stir

Never wonder about the time of return
For it's a long way to go
Never wonder about the way to return
For no one knows
The stars shall dim
And drift away
And leave the embrace

At least
Before falling
I can take a full breath of the wind
At least
I can gently knock
Towards the dawn's light
At least
I can hide a piece of blue
And say
I once soared in the clouds
