TEXT

等风来 等雨来
我在路上
等明天到来

数悲欢 百千度
星河高悬
即使道远日暮

梦里听见
你离去的脚步
回首已是
寂寂山河满目

是谁在云端
种下了一棵树
挂上你的名字
和万千祝福

雨会来 风会来
春日珊珊
守我恩慈满怀

此生铭记
这条漫漫长路

ENGLISH TRANSLATION

Waiting for the wind, waiting for the rain
I walk this road
Waiting for tomorrow's gain

Counting joys and sorrows, countless and deep
Stars hang high above
Though the road is long, the night is steep

In dreams, I hear
The footsteps of your flight
Looking back now
Silent mountains weep before my sight

Who's in the clouds
That planted a tree,
Bearing your name
And countless blessings free?

The rain will come, the wind will blow
Spring whispers softly
With kindness in tow

In this life, I'll remember this long, long road
This long, long road
